My Favorite Places

Here are a few of my favorite places and why I like them!

California

The entire state of California is a fun place, but I like it for a few spasific reasons:

  1. I get to see my extended family
  2. Seeing new wildlife on the hiking trails
  3. Going to Old Town Sacramento

Portland, Oregon

I grew up visiting Portland, and there are many reasons I still visit today!

  1. The Zoo is still one of the best zoo's I've seen
  2. Me and my mom see musicals every year at the theater
  3. Powell's Books is a very calming place to visit
Seattle, Washington

Seattle is a place I don't visit too often, but it's still a great place!

  1. The Space Needle is a very cool building (even if I'm afraid of heights!)
  2. The art museum there is very interesting
  3. Bainbridge Island is a really cool place to look around
